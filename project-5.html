Bench Oscilloscope

RP2350 Instrumentation

Project Overview

A custom-engineered bench oscilloscope and voltmeter powered by the robust RP2350 microcontroller. This instrument offers a lightweight but capable alternative for quick field diagnostics, electronics debugging, and rapid waveform analysis.

Core Functionality

  • High-speed ADC reading mapped directly to a graphical interface.
  • Dual-channel support for comparing signal timings.
  • Integrated software triggering and signal freezing.
  • Cost-effective instrumentation for hobbyist and academic applications.
